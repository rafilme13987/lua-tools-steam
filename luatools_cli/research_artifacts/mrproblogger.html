CHEAP DOMAIN REGISTRATION AND HOSTING

As the revolution in the IT sector, we all have eased to make our own business and profession on the digital platform. To spread our brand worldwide, we need to create our own business websites. But the creation of a website required two most important aspects, a Domain and a Hosting. A domain name is basically an identification of our business on the internet. Through the domain name (i.e. .com or .in or .net) business website gets established. But to access the domain we also need Hosting. Hosting is like a memory space of domain where all data of website are kept securely. For a beginner, it is a tough choice to make a decision about whether he should buy a cheap domain name and a hosting or not. Well, here we go with the context. Cheap domain registration and hosting seem likeable as well as attractive. As a fact, the lower you have to pay, the higher you will be interested in that. But everything has its pros and cons. Here we are going to discuss all the aspects of the pros and cons.

WHY WE SHOULD CHOOSE CHEAP DOMAIN REGISTRATION AND HOSTING? (PROS)
  1. Cost-Effectiveness: One of the most basic advantages is less cost. Also, they provide us with most of the accessible functions to maintain a website as a beginner. However, it depends upon the different domain and hosting providers.
  2. Blogging: Simple blogging can also regulate these services without any inconvenience. The concept of blogging is based on unique writing and posting. As they give sufficient space to do, it is a great advantage.
  3. The advantage for short term websites: For the many users who want to have a short term website. This is a good option for them, and also a great opportunity to grab. Domain registration gives them a cost-efficiency.
  4. Relevant for business and profession : It is relevant for business as well as a profession for making their credibility higher. In addition, it is a quick and cheap source to connect the worldwide audience.
WHY WE SHOULDN'T CHOOSE CHEAP DOMAIN REGISTRATION AND HOSTING? ( CONS)
  1. Professionalism: As a user of the website, many types of relevant features you won’t get. And the digital facilities that you want to serve your online audience will become difficult to develop.
  2. SEO unfriendly: It is difficult to win the trust of your visitors with a cheaper domain name because these type of domains are difficult to keep in mind as a user. Therefore, making your website famous online may be a harder task for you.
  3. LOSE Visitors and Rank: However, most of the relevant features will be missing. The visitors will not respond to the site at that level of enthusiasm they want to. Additionally, it affects the ranking of a website drastically.
  4. Customer Support: The cheapest domain registration and hosting providers has not good customer support. It is a very tough task to contact them directly and making yourself comfortable with their support. The more you pay the more you will be able to interact.
CONCLUSION:-

Now you are familiar to the pros and cons. It is totally based upon your requirement and what type of services are the necessity for you. Go ahead; have a cup of tea and make decisions to buy according to your need.
